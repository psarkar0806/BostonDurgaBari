OUR VISION

 

We are a group of young professionals with a passion to build a community in New England area.Our purpose is to promote ethnic culture, bring together people with a common interest and encourage a better way of living. We adore creativity, openness and take active interest for community well beings.

 

Durga Puja to us means a PANDAL, the organized chaos of worship and recitals, the festive cacophony of the backend kitchen, the aroma of khichuri bhog, dhunuchi naach, sindhur khela and dhaak. Most importantly it is the month long preparation itself.

 

This year we strive to recreate that KOLKATA essence of puja. We will put up a pandal, cook our own bhog, rejoice in the frenzy of dhunuchi naach.
 

Please join in the the preparation and the event itself. We need you more than ever, because we thrive together as a team.
